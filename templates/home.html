Our Mission

Founded in 2023 in Glasgow, Glasgow City Cycling wants to change how people in Glasgow commute. We observed that often times people are afraid of cycling on the road, and simply don't know where to start. Our mission is to bring the best gear to the people, and create a community of confident and responible cyclists in Glasgow.
